## Jiminy Cricket  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6  
> **Form:** Small Wooden or Metal Cricket  
> **Kind:** FAIRY TALE
  
Allows the user to retry a task that they failed within the past minute, using the same difficulty and modifiers.
